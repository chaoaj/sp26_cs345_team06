Puzzle concepts:
- platform based puzzles (tasks to complete to move onto the next section)
    ex: Fire boy and Water girl type of puzzles
- separate game based puzzles (a separate mini-game to complete to move on)
    ex: complete a maze that is separate from the sprite

Platform based:
- buttons that need to be pushed
- boxes to jump onto to for the sprite to reach platforms
- obtain keys to get through doors/gates
- player has to walk through portals
- controls are inverse for the player
- heavy/light gravity
- avoid "monsters"

Game based:
- maze
- "exit"/parking based game
    where there are other objects that can only move up/down & left/right
- laser
- sorting game
- memory game


General acceptance criteria:
- the player can understand what to do within 10-15 seconds without none to minimum
    amounts of directions/instructions
- there are interactive elements are visually distinct
- all puzzle interactions respond quickly to user input
- controls behave consistently with the rest of the platform
- the player must complete the puzzles to proceed
- the player can retry puzzles without restarting the entire level unless all lives are lost
- if a level is restarted, the puzzle must also restarted
